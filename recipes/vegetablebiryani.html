Vegetable Biryani

Vegetable biryani recipe

Vegetable Biryani is a flavourful rice dish made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, whole spices, freshly ginger, garlic, coriander and mint leaves.

If made by layering and cooking on dum, veg biryani is just as good as non-veg biryani.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes, cauliflower)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Soak for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, garam masala, turmeric, cumin, and red chili powder. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in mixed vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add rice, vegetable broth, and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until rice is cooked through.
  6. Fluff rice with a fork and stir in cilantro. Serve hot.
