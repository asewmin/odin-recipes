Butter Chicken

Butter chicken recipe

A super easy, full flavoured Butter Chicken recipe that rivals any restaurant.

Aromatic golden chicken pieces in an incredible curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece ginger, minced
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, marinate chicken with lemon juice, salt, garam masala, cumin, coriander, and cayenne pepper (if using) for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, heavy cream, and sugar. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with basmati rice or naan bread.
